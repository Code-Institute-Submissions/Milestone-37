Amsterdam's Top 3 Cafés

De Laatste Kruimel

In the heart of Amsterdam, you can enjoy fresh, traditional and “made with love” baked goods. From authentic scones, fresh bread, traditional quiches and pies, to tasty cakes and their baked “French toast” with cinnamon sugar. This can all be washed down with a delicious coffee. Their whole range is made from organic and regional, seasonal products, so you will want to enjoy every last crumb.

Winkel 43

At Noordermarkt in the Jordaan district, is a beautiful authentic Amsterdam corner building in which Winkel 43 is located. You can enjoy one of the sunniest terraces in Amsterdam from early in the morning until late in the evenings. They are famous for their apple pie, but they also offer a lunch and dinner/drinks menu that changes monthly. The menu is not complicated, but varied, tasty and reasonably priced.

Google map and reviews

Toki

Toki is a little café that opened in 2015. It is located in the center of Amsterdam right off of the popular shopping street, Haarlemmerdijk. Toki serves some of the tastiest coffee in Amsterdam as well as artisan tea accompanied by a small menu with fresh seasonal food, their banana bread being a local favorite. It’s a bright, trendy place to grab a quick coffee or have a catch up over a bite with friends.
